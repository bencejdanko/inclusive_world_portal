## What is the Portal?

The Inclusive World Portal is your one-stop place to:
- View and register for programs
- Track your attendance
- Stay connected with the community
- Complete important forms and surveys

## Your First Steps

### 1. Complete Your Profile

Your profile helps us get to know you better! To complete your profile:

1. Click on **Profile** in the sidebar menu
2. Fill in all the required information (marked with a red asterisk *)
3. Add a profile picture if you'd like
4. Click **Save** at the bottom

> **Tip:** A complete profile helps program coordinators match you with the best programs!

### 2. Fill Out Discovery Questions

The Discovery Questions help us understand your interests and needs:

1. Click on **Discovery Questions** in the sidebar
2. Answer all the questions honestly - there are no wrong answers!
3. Submit the form when you're done

### 3. Browse Programs

Once your profile and discovery questions are complete, you can:

1. Click on **Registration** to see available programs
2. Browse through different program options
3. Click on any program to see more details
4. Add programs you're interested in to your selection

## Need Help?

If you get stuck or have questions:
- Look for the **How To** section in the sidebar for more guides
- Contact your program coordinator
- Ask a staff member for assistance
