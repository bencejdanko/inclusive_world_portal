## Step-by-Step Registration

### Step 1: Browse the Program Catalog

1. Click on **Registration** in the sidebar menu
2. You'll see all available programs displayed as cards
3. Each card shows:
   - Program name
   - Schedule (days and times)
   - Location
   - Brief description

### Step 2: View Program Details

1. Click on any program card to see full details
2. Read about:
   - What the program is about
   - Who it's for
   - What you'll do
   - When and where it meets
3. Click **Add to Selection** if you're interested

### Step 3: Review Your Selection

1. After adding programs, click **Review Selection** or go to **Program Selection**
2. You'll see all the programs you've chosen
3. Review the total cost (if applicable)
4. Remove any programs you've changed your mind about

### Step 4: Complete Registration

1. Click **Proceed to Checkout**
2. Review your information
3. If payment is required:
   - Enter payment details
   - Click **Complete Registration**
4. You'll see a confirmation page when done!

## Important Notes

> **Registration Periods:** Registration is only open during specific times. If you see a lock icon, registration is currently closed.

> **Program Capacity:** Some programs fill up quickly! Register early to secure your spot.

> **Payment:** Members need to complete payment to finalize registration. Volunteers can register without payment.

## After Registration

Once you're registered:
- Check **Programs** to see all your enrolled programs
- View **My Attendance** to track your participation
- You'll receive notifications about your programs

## Troubleshooting

**Can't see the Registration option?**
- Make sure your profile and discovery questions are complete
- Check if registration is currently open

**Program not showing up?**
- Some programs may have specific requirements
- Contact your coordinator if you think you should have access
