### Notifications

1. Click on **Notifications** (the bell icon ðŸ””) in the sidebar
2. See important updates about:
   - Program changes
   - Schedule updates

### One Page Description (OPD)

Your OPD is a summary document about you:

1. Click on **One Page Description** in the sidebar
2. Review your information
3. This helps program staff support you better
4. Update it if anything changes

## Program Changes

### If You Need to Drop a Program

If you need to leave a program:
1. Contact your program coordinator directly
2. They can help you with the withdrawal process
3. Explain your reason so they can support you

### If Your Schedule Changes

Programs sometimes change times or locations:
- Check your notifications regularly
- Look at the Programs page for updates
- Contact your coordinator if you have conflicts

## Getting Help

### Who to Contact

- **Program Coordinator** - Questions about a specific program
- **Person-Centered Manager** - General support and guidance
- **Portal Support** - Technical issues with the website

### Common Questions

**Q: I can't see my program in the list**
A: Make sure your enrollment is confirmed. Check with your coordinator.

**Q: My attendance looks wrong**
A: Contact your program coordinator to review and update records.

**Q: How do I know about schedule changes?**
A: Check notifications regularly and keep your contact info updated.
