## What Are Notifications?

Notifications are messages that keep you updated about:
- Program announcements
- Schedule changes
- Attendance updates
- Important reminders
- Messages from coordinators

## Accessing Notifications

### Finding Your Notifications

1. Look for the **bell icon** (ðŸ””) in the sidebar
2. If you have unread notifications, you'll see a **red badge** with a number
3. Click on **Notifications** to view all messages

### The Notifications Page

When you open notifications, you'll see:
- **Unread** notifications at the top (highlighted)
- **Read** notifications below
- The date and time each notification was sent
- Who sent the notification

## Reading Notifications

### Viewing a Notification

1. Click on any notification to open it
2. Read the full message
3. The notification will automatically mark as "read"
4. The red badge number will decrease

## Managing Notifications

### Marking as Read

- Click on a notification to mark it as read
- Or click **Mark All as Read** to clear all unread notifications

### Deleting Notifications

If you want to remove old notifications:
1. Open the notification
2. Look for a delete or remove option
3. Confirm deletion

> **Note:** You can't undo deletion, so make sure you've read important info first!

## Notification Settings

### Staying Updated

To make sure you don't miss important notifications:
- Check the portal regularly (at least once a week)
- Keep your email address updated in your profile
- Some notifications may also be sent by email

### What to Do When You Get a Notification

1. **Read it promptly** - Some notifications are time-sensitive
2. **Take action if needed** - Follow any instructions in the message
3. **Ask questions** - Contact the sender if something is unclear
4. **Keep track** - Important notifications can be saved or noted

## Common Notification Scenarios

### Program Schedule Change

**What you'll see:**
> "The Art Class schedule has changed. New time: Wednesdays at 3:00 PM"

**What to do:**
- Check if the new time works for you
- Contact your coordinator if you have a conflict
- Update your personal calendar

### Attendance Confirmation

**What you'll see:**
> "Your attendance for Music Program on Nov 8 has been recorded"

**What to do:**
- Verify it's correct
- Contact coordinator if there's an error

### Important Reminder

**What you'll see:**
> "Reminder: Complete your monthly survey by Friday"

**What to do:**
- Complete the task before the deadline
- Mark notification as read when done

## Troubleshooting

**Not receiving notifications?**
- Check your profile settings
- Make sure your email is correct
- Ask staff to verify your notification settings

**Too many notifications?**
- Talk to your coordinator about adjusting notification frequency
- Focus on reading the most recent ones first
